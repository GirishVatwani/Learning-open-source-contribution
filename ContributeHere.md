## üõ† How to Contribute

To add your card to this page, follow these steps:

1. **Fork** this repo (claim your script).
2. **Copy** the [Card Template](#-card-template).
3. **Insert** your dialogue. 
   - *Pro Tip: Keep it to 3 cards per row to maintain the aesthetic.*
4. **Merge** your PR and take your place on the board.
5. **Submit a Pull Request!**
